If you aren’t keen on finance or if you are new to these kinds of charts, as I was, I’ll explain them briefly:

Each individual bars represent a day. The low point of the bars represent the low-point for the coin that day and so forth for the high/high point. The coloring represents where the coin closed that day in respect to the prior day. Red = below, Green = above.

The charts are interactive and they include a ‘rangeslider’ on the bottom that allows you to zoom into specific time-spans.
